# Description

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is technology enabling computers to simulate human intelligence, allowing them to learn, reason, solve problems, perceive, and understand language to perform tasks that typically need human intellect, like recognizing speech, making decisions, and generating content, often by learning from vast amounts of data
